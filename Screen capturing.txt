There are many shortcuts/tools you can use to capture your screen.

1) Press Windows Key + PrtSc to take a screenshot of your screen.
The pictures should be saved in This PC > Pictures > Screenshot

2) Use the 'Snipping Tool'. 
This should come installed with Windows 10 and can be found in the start menu.
You can use this tool to capture specific parts of a screen.

3) Use the 'Game bar'
The game bar is an Xbox app that allows you to record or broadcast your screen.
(You may need to enable this tool first in your settings)
Although it's meant for games you can use it to record your screen for anything.
Press Windows + Alt + R to start recording and press it again to stop recording.
Your files should be saved in This PC > Videos > Captures
